Methodology

Each app was installed from Google Play on a physical Android device and put through the five checks: airplane test (does it fully work offline?), permission check (what does it ask for — and does it hold the INTERNET permission?), tracker scan (Exodus Privacy report), policy audit (what the privacy policy admits), and exit test (can your files leave in a standard format, for free?). Device, app version and test date are noted per row. Pricing models are described, not judged — amounts change; models rarely do.

Full disclosure

QuietStor is our app. That's precisely why the checks are ones you can re-run yourself — nothing in our row requires believing us. And in fairness, here is what QuietStor doesn't do: no cloud sync of any kind (your safety copy is yours to store), no iOS version, and no app-icon disguise — we'd rather be a door than a fake calculator. If those are must-haves, one of the apps above may fit you better.

What the checks don't measure

Design taste, speed on your specific phone, and support quality. Try before you commit years of memories — and whichever vault you choose, run the exit test first. The best time to learn how you'd leave is before you move in.
