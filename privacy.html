Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Summary: QuietStor collects zero data. No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no network requests. Your files are encrypted locally and never leave your device.

1. Who We Are

QuietStor is developed and published by an independent developer based in the European Union. Contact: app.quietstor@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

None. QuietStor is designed with a zero-knowledge architecture:

  • No user accounts or registration
  • No analytics, telemetry, or usage tracking
  • No network requests — the app works 100% offline
  • No access to contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • No advertising SDKs or third-party trackers

3. Your Files & Encryption

All vault files are encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored exclusively on your device. Encryption keys are stored in the Android Keystore (hardware-backed TEE/StrongBox). We have no access to your files, your PIN, your encryption keys, or any metadata.

⚠️ Zero-knowledge means zero recovery. If you forget your PIN, your encrypted data is permanently lost. There is no "Forgot PIN" option, no backdoor, and no way for us to help recover your data.

4. Google Play Billing

If you purchase QuietStor Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.). We only receive a purchase confirmation token from Google to verify your Premium status locally on your device.

5. Crash Reporting (Opt-in Only)

QuietStor includes an optional, opt-in crash reporting feature powered by Firebase Crashlytics. This is disabled by default and only activated if you explicitly choose to help improve the app. Crash reports contain technical information only (device model, OS version, stack trace) — never file names, vault contents, or personal data.

6. Children's Privacy

QuietStor does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Since we collect no data at all, this concern is moot by design.

7. Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, DSGVO)

Since QuietStor collects no personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, delete, or port. If you wish to delete all local data, simply uninstall the app or use "Reset Vault" in Settings. For any questions, contact us at app.quietstor@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

For any privacy-related questions:
app.quietstor@gmail.com
