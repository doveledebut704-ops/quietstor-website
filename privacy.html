Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

Data Collection

QuietStor collects zero user data. No accounts, no analytics, no telemetry, no advertising identifiers. The app is 100% offline and makes no network requests.

Encryption

All vault files are encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored exclusively on your device. Encryption keys are stored in the Android Keystore (hardware-backed TEE/StrongBox). We have no access to your files, your password, your encryption keys, or any metadata.

⚠️ Zero-knowledge means zero recovery. If you forget your password, your encrypted data is permanently lost. There is no "Forgot Password" option, no backdoor, and no way for us to help recover your data.

Optional Crashlytics

QuietStor includes Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting, disabled by default. If you choose to enable it in Settings, only crash reports (stack traces, device model, OS version) are sent. No file content, names, passwords, or encryption keys are ever included.

Google Play Billing

Premium purchases are processed by Google Play. We do not receive or store any payment information. Purchase verification is done locally on device.

Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations, you have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Since we collect none, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Your data exists only on your device, under your control.

Questions? Contact us at app.quietstor@gmail.com
