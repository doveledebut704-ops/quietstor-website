Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

QuietStor is a privacy-first file manager. Your files stay on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data.

Data Collection

QuietStor does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • File contents or metadata
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Data Storage

All data remains locally on your device. QuietStor does not use cloud storage or external servers.

Third-Party Services

QuietStor does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

QuietStor requests only the permissions necessary to manage files on your device. These permissions are used solely for local file operations.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@quietstor.app
