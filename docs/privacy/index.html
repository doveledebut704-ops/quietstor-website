Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

QuietStor ("we", "our", "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data.

QuietStor is designed to work 100% offline. Your files never leave your device. We have no servers, no accounts, no cloud storage.

3. Crash Reports

We use Firebase Crashlytics to collect anonymous crash reports. These reports help us fix bugs and improve the app. They contain:

  • Device model and Android version
  • App version
  • Technical crash information (stack traces)

Crash reports never include: file names, file paths, PIN codes, or any content from your vault.

4. Local Storage

QuietStor stores data locally on your device:

  • Encrypted files in the vault (AES-256-GCM)
  • App preferences (theme, language)
  • Vault settings (encrypted)

This data is never transmitted to any server.

5. Third-Party Services

QuietStor uses Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting only. No other third-party services are used. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Data Security

Your vault files are encrypted using:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption
  • PBKDF2 key derivation (600,000 iterations)
  • Android Keystore for key protection

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our servers. All your data is on your device and under your control.

8. Children's Privacy

QuietStor does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app contains no features that target children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

10. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: quietstor.app@gmail.com
