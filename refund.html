Refund Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Our Promise: We want you to be completely satisfied with QuietStor. If you're not happy, we'll help you get a refund.

1. Google Play Refund Policy

QuietStor subscriptions are processed through Google Play:

  • Within 48 hours: You can request a refund directly through Google Play for any reason.
  • After 48 hours: Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

2. How to Request a Refund

Option 1: Through Google Play

  1. Go to Google Play Subscriptions
  2. Find QuietStor in your subscriptions
  3. Click "Cancel subscription" and follow the refund prompts

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

Email us at app.quietstor@gmail.com with:

  • Your Google Play order number (starts with GPA...)
  • The email address used for the purchase
  • Reason for the refund request

We will respond within 48 hours.

3. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Note: Canceling a subscription does not automatically issue a refund. If you want a refund, you must request it separately.

4. Free Trial

If QuietStor offers a free trial, you will not be charged if you cancel before the trial ends.

5. Contact

For questions about refunds: app.quietstor@gmail.com
