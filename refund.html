Refund Policy

Last updated: February 16, 2026

QuietStor Premium is a one-time purchase of €1.99 / $1.99. There is no subscription, no recurring charge, and no auto-renewal. You pay once and keep Premium forever.

1. Purchase Model

QuietStor Premium is a one-time, non-recurring purchase made through Google Play Billing. Once purchased, Premium features are unlocked permanently on your Google account. There is nothing to cancel because there is no subscription.

2. Refund via Google Play

All purchases are processed by Google Play. To request a refund:

  • Within 48 hours of purchase: request directly through Google Play order history
  • After 48 hours: contact us at app.quietstor@gmail.com with your order number, and we will review your request

3. What Happens After a Refund

If a refund is granted, your Premium features will be deactivated. Your vault files remain encrypted and accessible with the free tier's 500 MB limit. No data is deleted.

4. Restore Purchase

If you reinstall QuietStor or switch to a new device, use the "Restore Purchase" button in the app. It verifies your purchase with Google Play — no need to buy again.

5. Contact

For any billing questions: app.quietstor@gmail.com
