Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-16

Google Play Policy

QuietStor Premium is sold through Google Play. Refund requests are handled by Google's standard refund process.

Within 48 Hours

Google Play automatically allows refunds within 48 hours of purchase. Go to Google Play → Account → Order History → QuietStor → Refund.

After 48 Hours

Contact us at support@quietstor.app explaining the issue. We'll help where possible, but note that Google controls the final decision on refunds outside the 48-hour window.

What We Cannot Refund

Data loss due to a forgotten password is not grounds for a refund. QuietStor uses zero-knowledge encryption — this is clearly documented before purchase.

Consumer Mediation (EU)

If no satisfactory solution is reached, EU consumers may refer the dispute to a consumer mediator or use the EU Online Dispute Resolution platform. See our Terms of Service § 10 for details.

Need help? support@quietstor.app

Politique Google Play

QuietStor Premium est vendu via Google Play. Les remboursements suivent la politique standard de Google.

Dans les 48 heures
