Everything you need to know about QuietStor.

General

QuietStor is a private photo vault for Android: photos, videos and documents, encrypted with AES-256 on your device. 100% offline — no cloud, no account, no tracking. Free to start; one purchase unlocks everything, no subscription.
Yes! Free includes:
  • 1 private space
  • View & play everything, always
  • PIN + biometric unlock
  • Protected ZIP export (Keep) + import
  • Safety copy + recovery phrase
  • Add up to 200 items
Premium (one purchase, no subscription) unlocks:
  • Everything in Free
  • Unlimited adds
  • Multiple spaces + locked sub-vault
  • Private camera
  • Give a sealed copy by one-time code
  • Intruder selfie + Panic PIN
  • Themes, icons & stickers
The free limit only applies to adding. Viewing, exporting and recovering what's already yours stays free — no ransom, ever.

Vault & Security

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (chunked streaming, 1 MB/chunk)
  • Key storage: Android Keystore (TEE/StrongBox hardware)
  • Per-file keys: HKDF derivation with unique 32-byte salt
  • Password hashing: PBKDF2 with 600,000 iterations
  • Database: SQLCipher with derived passphrase
When you create your vault, QuietStor gives you a 12-word recovery phrase. Forgot your PIN? The phrase restores access — on this phone or a new one. What we can't do is recover anything for you: zero-knowledge means we never see your PIN, your phrase or your files. So write the phrase down somewhere safe, and keep a safety copy of your vault. With both, you can always come back.

Export & Backup

Premium users can export their vault as a standard encrypted ZIP file protected by a strong password (8+ characters, at least 1 letter + 1 digit — different from your vault password).

The ZIP can be opened with 7-Zip, WinRAR, macOS Finder, or any tool that supports AES-256 ZIP encryption. No proprietary format, no lock-in.
1. Export vault as ZIP on old phone → 2. Transfer ZIP (USB, SD card, etc.) → 3. Install QuietStor on new phone → 4. Import ZIP → 5. Enter export password. Done.

Data & Privacy

No. Zero data collection. No analytics, no accounts, no telemetry. Optional opt-in Crashlytics for bug reports only — disabled by default.
