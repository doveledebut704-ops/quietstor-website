// Your files, your way

Two ways out. Zero lock-in.

Most vault apps trap your files in a proprietary format. QuietStor gives you the choice — both protected by a password you set.

Encrypted Backup

Your vault in your cloud

Like carrying your safe to the cloud. Upload to Google Drive, Dropbox, or any storage — your files stay fully encrypted and unreadable, even if the cloud is hacked. When you need them back, import into QuietStor and unlock with your password.

  • AES-256 encryption — military grade
  • Safe on any cloud, any USB drive
  • Unreadable without QuietStor + your password
  • Perfect for secure backups
Best for: cloud backups, device transfers, maximum security

Protected ZIP

Portable and protected

A standard password-protected ZIP with your original files inside. Open it with 7-Zip, WinRAR, or macOS by entering your password. No QuietStor needed — your files go wherever you go, and only you can open them.

  • Password-protected ZIP standard
  • Open with 7-Zip, WinRAR, macOS
  • Original files, untouched
  • No special app needed
Best for: sharing, moving to a new phone, leaving QuietStor with all your data

Our promise: Your files are never trapped. Maximum security for the cloud, or universal access for the world — both protected, you choose.
